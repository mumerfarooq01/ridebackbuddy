RideBack Buddy

The Canadian designated-driver service that gets you and your car home safely. A warm, dependable brand built on trust, care, and a friend-who-shows-up feeling.

Positioning Statement

For Canadians who went out and can’t drive back, RideBack Buddy is a two‑driver service that brings you and your own car safely home — so your night ends in your driveway, not a parking lot.

Voice & Tone

We sound like

  • A friend with keys in hand
  • Calm, dependable, a little playful
  • Canadian-polite, not corporate
  • Safety‑first without being preachy

Do say

  • “We’ll drive you home in your car.”
  • “Two drivers, one simple fare.”
  • “Park it safe — in your driveway.”

Don’t say

  • “Anti‑DUI solution” (preachy)
  • “Chauffeur service” (wrong tier)
  • “Disruptive platform” (corporate)
