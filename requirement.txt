Build a modern, professional Next.js 14 website for "Ride Home Designated Drivers" — 
a designated driver service operating in Mississauga, Oakville, Burlington, Hamilton, 
and the GTA (Ontario, Canada).

---

## 🎨 Design Style
- Dark, premium feel with deep navy (#0B1120) as the base background
- Accent color: bright red (#E63946) for CTAs and highlights
- Secondary accent: gold/amber (#F4A261) for trust badges and icons
- Clean white text on dark backgrounds
- Font: Inter or Geist Sans
- Mobile-first, fully responsive
- Subtle animations using Framer Motion (fade-in on scroll, button hover effects)

---

### 2. Services & Pricing Page (/services)
- Hero with title "Our Services & Pricing"
- 4 detailed service sections with icons, descriptions, and use-case bullet points:

  **Standard DD Service**
  - $31.00 minimum (first 10 km)
  - $2.75 per additional km
  - $1.00/min standby after 5-min grace
  - Flat rates for 50km+
  - 407-ETR charges extra if applicable

  **Special Events & Weddings**
  - Group & standby rates available
  - Suitable for: Christmas parties, corporate events, weddings, golf tournaments
  - Reservation requires valid credit card

  **Vehicle Drop Service**
  - Drop car to mechanic, airport, detailer
  - Perfect for vacations or busy schedules

  **Medical & Senior Service**
  - Post-procedure transportation
  - Multi-stop errands (pharmacy, grocery, salon)
  - Mobility assistance door-to-door

- Pricing FAQ accordion at the bottom

---

### 3. Booking Page (/book)
- Multi-step booking form (3 steps):

  **Step 1 — Ride Details**
  - Service type selector (dropdown or card selection):
    - Standard Designated Driver
    - Special Event / Wedding
    - Vehicle Drop
    - Medical / Senior
  - Pickup date & time (date picker)
  - Pickup address (text input)
  - Drop-off address (text input)
  - Estimated distance (auto or manual input)
  - Special notes / instructions

  **Step 2 — Personal Info**
  - Full name
  - Phone number
  - Email address
  - Number of passengers
  - Any accessibility needs? (yes/no toggle)

  **Step 3 — Review & Confirm**
  - Summary of all entered details
  - Estimated fare calculator based on distance:
    - Under 10 km → $31.00
    - Over 10 km → $31.00 + (extra km × $2.75)
  - Disclaimer: "Fare is an estimate. Final price confirmed by dispatcher."
  - Submit button → shows confirmation message:
    "Thank you! Your request has been received. 
     A dispatcher will call to confirm your booking."

- Form validation on all fields (react-hook-form + zod)
- Progress indicator at the top (Step 1 / 2 / 3)
- Smooth step transitions with Framer Motion
