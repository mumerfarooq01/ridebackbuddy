Answers

Common questions.

Is my car actually insured?

Yes. Your own policy provides primary coverage. Our commercial hired-auto policy sits on top for liability and damage while our driver is at the wheel. Plain-English details are in the Terms link at booking.

Why two drivers?

One drives your car, one drives our chase car to collect the first driver afterward. It also means you are never alone in a stranger’s vehicle — safer for everyone.

What if I drive a manual transmission or a Tesla?

Just tell us at booking. We match you with a driver trained on that vehicle. Manuals, EVs, and even classic cars are fine.

How is this different from Uber or a taxi?

Rideshare and taxis pick you up in their car and leave yours behind. We use your car. You wake up with it in the driveway. That’s the whole idea.

Do you serve all of Canada?

We’re launching city by city, starting with a pilot metro in 2026. Enter your postal code in the app to see live coverage — and to request service if we’re not there yet.

Can I book for someone else?

Absolutely. Enter their name and phone number at booking and pay on your card. Common for parents, spouses, or corporate accounts covering employees.

What’s your cancellation policy?

Free to cancel up to 5 minutes after booking. After that, a flat $15 cancellation applies so our drivers (already on the way) are paid for their time.
